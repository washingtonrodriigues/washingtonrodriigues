## ♦ About me:

I'm Washington Rodrigues and i'm 28. I work as Lead Success Engineer at Barte, a Brazilian Corporate Banking and payment infrastructure company, only 4 years old and already valued at approximately R$1 billion. I am responsible for the technical aspects of Success, directly involved in client onboarding, reducing time-to-value, and accelerating revenue generation through efficient platform activation.

My work involves hands-on integration, technical support, advanced troubleshooting, and architecture, as well as supporting response times for more complex technical demands. My focus is on removing technical friction, increasing operational efficiency, and ensuring that clients can operate and scale the product as quickly as possible.
